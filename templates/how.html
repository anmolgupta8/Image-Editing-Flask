How to Use Edit Monkey

Edit Monkey provides a simple and intuitive platform for editing images online. Follow the steps below to use Edit Monkey effectively.

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Start by clicking on the "Choose file" button and select the image you want to edit. Edit Monkey supports various image formats such as PNG, JPEG, WebP, and more.

Step 2: Select an Operation

Choose the desired operation from the dropdown menu. Options include converting to PNG, grayscale, WebP, and JPEG formats. Select the operation that fits your editing needs.

Step 3: Click Submit

After selecting the operation, click the "Submit" button. Edit Monkey will process your image and provide a link to the edited version.

Step 4: View and Download

Once the processing is complete, you can view the edited image by clicking on the provided link. If satisfied, download the edited image to your device.

Example:

As an example, let's convert a colorful image to grayscale. Follow the steps above, select the "Convert to Grayscale" operation, and observe the transformation.
